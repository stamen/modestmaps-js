Modest Maps is a family of minimalist map API's. The goal is to provide a minimal, extensible, customizable, and free basis for mapping.

Our intent is to provide a small and extensible library for designers and developers who want to use interactive maps in their own projects. Modest Maps provides a core set of features in a tight, clean package with plenty of hooks for additional functionality.
